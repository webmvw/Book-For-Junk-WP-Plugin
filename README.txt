Easily schedule junk removal with this plugin. Book pickup times, manage waste disposal, and streamline junk hauling services for a cleaner, clutter-free space.

== Description ==

The "Book for Junk" plugin simplifies junk removal and waste management by allowing users to easily schedule pickup times for their unwanted items. Whether you're clearing out clutter, renovating, or need regular trash services, this tool provides a seamless way to book junk removal services. With an easy-to-use interface, you can select the best time for a pickup, manage your waste disposal needs, and ensure eco-friendly disposal of your items. Perfect for both homeowners and businesses, the plugin helps streamline the process of hauling junk, keeping your space clean and clutter-free with minimal effort.


== Installation ==

1. Upload `book-for-junk.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Place `<?php do_action('plugin_name_hook'); ?>` in your templates


== It's not working == 

- Step 1. Check if your Plugin is activated.
- Step 2. Deactivate all plugins and reactivate Book for junk plugin


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

- How do I schedule a junk pickup? -
To schedule a junk pickup, simply navigate to the plugin’s booking page, select your preferred pickup time, and submit your request. You’ll receive a confirmation with the scheduled time and details. 

- What types of junk can I schedule for pickup? -
You can schedule pickups for household junk, old furniture, appliances, yard waste, and other non-hazardous items. If you have specific questions about what can be collected, please refer to our guidelines or contact support.

- Is the junk removed in an environmentally friendly way? -
Yes! We are committed to responsible disposal. Whenever possible, your junk will be recycled, reused, or donated, helping to reduce environmental impact.
